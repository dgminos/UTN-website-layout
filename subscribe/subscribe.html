SUBSCRIBE

We invite you to join us as we explore what the various coffee growing regions around the world have to offer. Join our Coffee Club giving you access to some of the world’s best coffees. Each month we will deliver our coffee of the month, hand-picked by our Master Roaster Dumo Mathema.

You decide how often you would like to receive 250g of coffee and whether you want whole bean or a bespoke grind, we will ship direct to your home or business.

“We have sourced over 200 single origin coffees since 2010, and we will continue to do so for you.”

We're excited to get your coffee to you and ship using our shipping partner Royal Mail who is currently experiencing a large increase in demand due to the pandemic. We ask that you allow 3-7 working days for your order to arrive.


We ship orders on weekdays only, if your order falls on a weekend we’ll pick this up on our next roasting day.


Answers to any questions you may have about our service can be answered by clicking the button below and reading our Subscriptions FAQ page. If you still have further questions, please see our contact us at shop@departmentofcoffee.com.
