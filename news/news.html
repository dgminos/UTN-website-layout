SAME GREAT COFFEE, BRAND NEW FOOD MENU

We are so excited to announce we are opening! Our Carnaby Street coffee shop is opening on Friday 17th September at 9am. We have a new delicious and exciting menu full of fun and flavour to be enjoyed with our great coffee!


WHATS NEW?

We're kicking things off with a brand new all day menu, there's breakfast, brunch, lunch and so much more for you to enjoy. We have a new range of freshly baked pastries, toasties, bagels and brunch dishes. We are so excited to be introducing our ultimate favourite coffee food companion … pancakes & American waffles! Our perfectly fluffy, sweet, melt in your mouth pancakes or the warm, deliciously sweet and crunchy American waffles are just the beginning. Now make it your own, and choose your toppings. You can add fresh juicy blueberries, sliced banana, chocolatey nutella, sweet honey, maple syrup, and streaky bacon. Have one or have them all, it's up to you! Paired perfectly with a coffee and you have yourself a match made in heaven.
